About me

My Information

My name is Denilson Makotsi Imbuka, a highly skilled Construction Management professional based in Nairobi, Kenya. With a Bachelor of Technology in Construction and Property Management from Kirinyaga University, I possess extensive experience in project management, quality control, and team leadership. I am proficient in various aspects of construction operations, including building design, carpentry, electrical works, and property maintenance.

Currently, I serve as a Property and Facilities Officer at Absa Bank PLC HQ under NW Realite, where I oversee day-to-day facility operations, manage external service providers, and ensure compliance with safety regulations. My passion lies in delivering quality projects, managing teams, and streamlining operations to meet client needs.
